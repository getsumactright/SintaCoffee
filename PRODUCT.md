# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Couples planning weddings, corporate event organizers, and private party hosts in Northern California (Solano County, Napa Valley, Sacramento, and the Bay Area). They want to provide a premium, memorable beverage experience for their guests with zero logistics stress.

## Product Purpose
To showcase Sinta Coffee Co.'s mobile specialty coffee cart, detailing packages and pricing, and driving inquiries through a high-converting, personalized booking/quote request form. The website needs to immediately convey exceptional hospitality and the artisanal quality of the coffee.

## Brand Personality
- **Warm & Sophisticated**: Friendly, hospitable, and deeply personal (inspired by "sinta" meaning beloved).
- **Artisanal & Precise**: Obsessed with quality (husband-and-wife team with 20+ years of coffee industry expertise), represented by elegant typography and smooth layout transitions.
- **Cinematic**: Immersive storytelling through video-based loading sequences and a polished, stone/cream visual theme.
- **Three-word personality**: Hospitable, Handcrafted, Cinematic.

## Anti-references
- **Standard SaaS Landing Pages**: Clean, sterile white/light-gray backgrounds with blue buttons and generic icon grids.
- **Lazy AI Template Styles**: Overuse of rounded cards (radii > 16px), floating drop shadows on everything, and tiny tracked uppercase eyebrows above every section.
- **Generic Coffee Sites**: Overly saturated pictures of brown beans, chalk board textures, and fake handwritten font scripts.

## Design Principles
1. **Cinematic Storytelling**: Welcome visitors with an immersive, high-quality video loading animation that introduces the founders and their craft, setting a warm, personal tone from the start.
2. **Tactile Color Pacing**: Guide visitors through a clean, smooth interface using natural, video-derived tones (Pebble Stone, Cotton Cream, Deep Espresso) for a grounded, organic feel.
3. **Conversational Connection**: Retain details like the virtual host mascot widget to showcase Maeme & Benicia, building trust and personal connection.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG AA Target**: Ensure minimum contrast of 4.5:1 for body copy on the cream/stone canvases.
- **Reduced Motion Support**: Ensure all transitions and loaders respect `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` by bypassing the video intro and rendering the site layout instantly.
- **Keyboard & Screen Reader Accessible**: Proper semantic HTML structure and form fields with clear visual focus outlines.
